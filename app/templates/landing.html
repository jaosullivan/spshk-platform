Our Story

Over a century of Irish community in Hong Kong

St Patrick's Society Hong Kong was founded in 1931 by a small group of Irish merchants and civil servants who wanted to keep alive the traditions of home in a city that was fast becoming their second country.

Today, with over 2,000 members drawn from every corner of Ireland and the global Irish diaspora, SPSHK is the oldest continuously active Irish society in Asia — and still very much the social home of the Irish community in Hong Kong.

We host everything from the Annual Ball and beach barbecues to quiz nights, treasure hunts, horseracing days, and Irish language classes. Whatever brings you closer to the people and traditions you love about Ireland, you'll find it here.
