# How to Sign Up for St. Patrick's Society Hong Kong Membership

Welcome to the St. Patrick's Society of Hong Kong! Follow these simple steps to register for your lifetime membership, set up your online login, and securely complete your payment.

## Prerequisites
Before you begin the application, make sure you have the following information ready:
* First Name
* Surname (Last Name)
* Email Address
* A secure password for account login
* A valid credit/debit card for the membership fee

## Membership Fee
* **Cost**: HKD $888 (One-time lifetime fee)
* **Payment Processor**: Stripe (Secure external payment gateway)

---

## Step-by-Step Registration Instructions

### Step 1: Navigate to the Official Membership Page
1. Open your web browser.
2. Go directly to the [St. Patrick's Society HK Membership Page](https://stpatrickshk.com).

### Step 2: Complete the Application Form
1. Scroll down to the **Green Card Membership** sign-up section.
2. Enter your **First Name** into the designated field.
3. Enter your **Surname** into the Last Name field.
4. Input your primary **Email Address** to ensure you receive confirmation and monthly newsletter updates.

### Step 3: Create Your Password
1. Locate the account security section on the form.
2. Type your chosen password into the **Password** field.
3. Re-enter the password in the **Confirm Password** field to ensure accuracy.
*Note: This password will be required to log into the portal to access member-only content and loyalty perks after registration.*

### Step 4: Secure Stripe Payment Execution
1. Review the order summary showing the total amount due: **$888 HKD**.
2. Locate the secure Stripe credit card form at the bottom of the registration screen.
3. Enter your **Card Number**, **Expiry Date (MM/YY)**, and **CVC/CVV code**.
4. Check the box to accept the terms, conditions, and privacy policy if prompted.
5. Click the **Pay & Register** button. 
*Note: Do not refresh your browser or click the back button while the Stripe transaction processing animation is active.*

### Step 5: Order Confirmation
1. Upon successful payment, a confirmation page will display your new **Membership ID**.
2. Check your registered personal email inbox for two automated emails:
   * A payment receipt generated directly from Stripe.
   * A welcome onboarding email from the St. Patrick's Society.

---

## Logging in After Registration
Once your application is submitted successfully, you can access your profile at any time:
1. Return to the homepage and click **Log In**.
2. Enter your registered **Email Address** and the **Password** created during step 3.

## Password Reset Instructions
If you forget your password, you can recover access using your registered email address:
1. Navigate to the portal **Log In** page.
2. Click the **Forgot Password?** or **Reset Password** link below the login fields.
3. Enter your **registered personal email address** when prompted.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. Open your personal email inbox and look for a message from the society.
6. Click the secure verification link inside the email to choose a new password.

## Membership Benefits
Once registered as a Green Card member, you will receive:
* **Event Invitations**: Exclusive invitations to major functions like the Annual Ball, beach barbecues, and quiz nights.
* **Monthly Newsletter**: Automated updates on upcoming Irish cultural activities across Hong Kong.
* **Local Discounts**: 10-15% discount for two people at selected bars and restaurants throughout the city.

## Support and Enquiries
If you experience any technical difficulties during the signup process, if your Stripe payment fails, or if you do not receive your reset link, please contact the society administrators directly via email at info@stpatrickshk.com.
